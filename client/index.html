The Story

The Last Island

When the old world drowned, the ocean kept only one scrap of land above the tide — a single restless island that surfaces somewhere new with every storm.

The last survivors wash ashore with empty hands and one shared instinct: the island keeps just one of you. Scavenge guns from the wreckage, patch yourself together, and outlast everyone as the safe ground shrinks beneath your feet.

Every match is a new tide and a new island. Drop in, gear up, and be the last one standing.

The Objective

How to Play

Last survivor wins. Simple to learn, brutal to master.

1

Drop & Scavenge

You start with nothing but your fists. Loot guns, ammo and healing from crates scattered across the wreckage.

2

Gear Up

Carry up to four weapons, stock healing items and pick the gun that fits the fight — close range or long shots.

3

Survive the Shrink

The safe zone closes in. Stay inside the ring or take damage as the map forces everyone together.

4

Be the Last One

Outplay every other survivor. The island keeps just one of you — make sure it's you.

⚠

Watch the Red Zones

Outside the safe circle the ground turns into a red zone — a deadly storm that drains your health the longer you stay in it. On the minimap it shows as a red area; the white ring is the shrinking safe zone. Always move toward the safe zone before it closes — get caught in the red and it will kill you faster than any enemy.
